## §A Executive product-truth verdict

1. **Rooms now feel meaningfully different in reality, not just in doctrine**, with one clear exception (Stillness, which is intentionally spare and now sits on the edge of repetitive). Release reads distinctly somatic, Growth reads distinctly cultivational, Clarity reads distinctly discerning, Joy reads distinctly offering-register, and Connection reads distinctly devotional after Phase B Bhakti authoring. **Sampled**:

**Verdict**: **YES, distinctly different places.**

**Why (quoted text)**:
- Stillness voice is *pre-cognitive, non-demanding*: "Evenings can leave the body still racing. Warm it, slow it, let it land." The banner register is sākṣī-witness / vāta-light.
